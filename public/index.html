What is MDIT X DvD 2023 ?

Malaysia Data Innovation Talent x DOSM Virtual Datathon 2023 (MDIT x DvD 2023) is a national competition for students from IPTA and IPTS held once every two years to explore and sharpen participants skills in data innovation. MDIT x DvD 2023 focuses on enhancing the use of big data, encouraging participant's critical thinking in data analysis and problem- solving. Participants from various disciplines will work on open data projects and showcase data analysis skills and talents. MDIT x DVD 2023 serves as a pedestal for students to collaborate and engage with diverse stakeholders to impact Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) progress significantly.

We welcome the energetic, ambitious, creative, and innovative participants to join us today!

Organisers

Kelab Akademik Statistik (inStats) Pusat Pengajian Sains dan Matematik
UiTM Shah Alam
Department of Statistic Malaysia (DOSM)
