What is MDIT 2023 ?

Malaysia Data Innovation Talent 2023 (MDIT 2023) is a national competition for students from IPTA and IPTS held once every two years to explore and sharpen participants skills in data innovation. MDIT 2023 focuses on enhancing the use of big data, encouraging participant's critical thinking in data analysis and problem- solving. Participants from various disciplines will work on open data projects and showcase data analysis skills and talents. MDIT 2023 serves as a pedestal for students to collaborate and engage with diverse stakeholders to impact Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) progress significantly.

We welcome the energetic, ambitious, creative, and innovative participants to join us today!

15 JUNE 2023

Participation Registration Begin

Registration is open for program participants according to the requirements set by the organizers through the official platform of MDIT 2023.

1 AUGUST 2023

Participation Registration End

Registration is closed for program participants according to requirement set by the organizers through the official platform of MDIT 2023.

23 SEPTEMBER 2023

Opening Ceremony

The ceremony will be conducted virtually and open to all students from the university


Competition Briefing

Participants must attend the competition briefing slot as scheduled. This session is tailored for every participant and will be conducted by Dr. Ahmad Fazreen Bin Hj. Baharuden, an instructor from BIGIT with 20 years of background expertise in corporate management, busines development, data analytics, project management, performance management, andbusiness analysis.
Participants will be briefed about MDIT 2023, from objectives and concepts to rules and regulations in this slot. It is an opportunity for each team to clarify any MDIT 2023 related concerns further


Data Visualization Workshop using Power BI

Power BI is a business analytics service that connects to and visualizes any data using the unified, scalable platform for self service and enterprise business intelligence (BI) that is easy to use and helps you gain quick and deeper data insight.

Power BI offers an excellent query editor tool that will help in modeling, visualizing, and analyzing data and help you create a good report. In this workshop, a professional trainer will help to assist all participants on how to utilize Power BI as the medium for their solutions. The 2 hours session will include the perks of Power BI to visualize and analyze data in customized interactive dashboards

24 SEPTEMBER 2023

Pitching Workshop

Pitching is not just about telling, it is selling and convincing investors of how well your products can get through the market, in a simple and precise way. The idea of pitching is to attract attention towards the marketability of your productions.


This workshop is specifically for participants of MDIT2023 as their guidance on how to deliver effective pitching. We invite a professional trainer, that will convey the art of pitching and how to apply it in this competition. The additional value of the pitching workshop is to ensure that participants are ready to compete with a brief but meaningful and informative pitch.

25 SEPTEMBER 2023 - 1 OCTOBER 2023

Project Submission

Participants need to submit their final product in the form of a dashboard along with their video recording of their findings critically and creatively on the official MDIT2023 platform. Their final products and videos will be judged by 3 professional jurors from a wide variety of background fields and expertise.

14 OCTOBER

Top 10 Announcement

The top 10 groups will be announced on the official MDIT2023 platform as finalists and they must proceed to the next session with the same members and projects.

20 OCTOBER

Top 10 Data Pitching

The final round will be held physically, venue which will be announced later through MDIT 2023 official platform. With the same group members, project and final product, they will have to present their findings live and will be judged by 3 other professional juries brought specially from selected industries and companies.

21 OCTOBER

Closing Ceremony

The ceremony will be conducted physically and open to all students from the university involved with the MDIT2023 program. Winner announcement session will also be announced at this session.
